About Me

I am currently a full-time ninja at Coding Dojo based in Los Angeles and I am more than happy to stay confined within my bedroom walls as long as I need to, in order to grow and learn more about coding. I am fully committed and determined to seeing these goals all the way through, for I hope to eventually become a Full Stack Web Developer in the near future.

When I am not coding, I spend my time feeding my passion for music by djing and playing instruments like the piano and guitar. Although I enjoy being able to spend time on my hobbies indoors, I also love to get out of the house to experience the outside world. This includes traveling to beautiful destinations for camping, taking my dog out with me to the water on my paddleboard, or even just going to the local park to shoot some hoops.
